Incremental theme

The object measures out the event

Mary ate the apple — eat half the apple, do half the eating

Krifka 1989/1998 · Dowty 1991
μ : theme → event (homomorphism)
The incremental theme sets up a structure-preserving map μ from the parts of the apple to the parts of the eating-event: each portion of apple consumed corresponds to exactly that much event. When the apple is gone, the event culminates — its telic endpoint.
