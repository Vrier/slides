4Modal scope & representationFormal6 marks

For Clara might marry a violinist, the de re form is \(\exists x[\text{violinist}(x,w_0)\wedge\exists w'(\textsc{Acc}(w_0,w')\wedge\text{marry}(c,x,w'))]\).

(a)Give the de dicto representation. What changes between the two formulas? In which world is violinist evaluated on each reading?
(b)For Jonah believes that a reviewer praised \(p_j\), the de re form is \(\exists x[\text{reviewer}(x,w_0)\wedge\forall w'(\textsc{Bel}_j(w_0,w')\to\text{praised}(x,p_j,w'))]\). Give the de dicto form, and say whether the individual must be fixed in \(w_0\) on both readings.
